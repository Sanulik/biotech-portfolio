Written Summary

The project focused on Viome, a biotechnology company that utilizes advanced metatranscriptomic sequencing technology to provide personalized health insights. Unlike traditional DNA tests that provide static genetic predispositions, Viome analyzes RNA to determine active gene expression within the gut microbiome.

The video presentation detailed the complete product journey: beginning with the initial sample collection and moving through the lab analysis phase, where supercomputers process vast amounts of biological data. A key highlight of the project was the "Viome App," which translates complex microbial data into actionable dietary recommendations.
