Session Evidence

Figure 1: Group 03 during the session.

Reflection: For this group presentation, I was responsible for explaining the "Code to Cure" workflow. I used the analogy of a "Super-Brain" and "Tiny Tech" to illustrate how we move from data analysis to physical cellular treatment. The primary challenge was adhering to the "Do's and Don'ts" of the guidelines, specifically explaining the slides rather than reading them and maintaining eye contact without using notes. I had to ensure my voice was loud and confident enough to engage the entire class. This assignment improved my ability to simplify high-level biotechnology for a general audience. It also taught me the importance of visual creativity in slides (such as using the Iron Man/Super Soldier Serum theme) to motivate and capture the audience's interest.
